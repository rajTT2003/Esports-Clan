Valorant

Rudi "Rudi" Mcken

18 year old Rudi started his Valorant career in June 2021. Since then he has risen through the ranks and is now one of the top players in his respective scene. Also known as Afro Man, everyone knows when Rudi enters the room!

Samuel "SteMpy" Stemp

24 year old SteMpy started his Esports career in the early days of Counter Strike Global Offensive. Fighting his was up to ESEA Main he made a name for himself in the UK CS Scene. Moving to Valorant in late 2021 he has made solidified himself as the loudest Valorant player at LAN.

Niklas "Pika" Holze

19 year old Pika was the final piece to the puzzle. Starting his Valorant career in early 2022, making a name for himself whilst playing for the world-renowned Pablo's Kittens. Being the newest player on the team he has quickly shown that he is ready for anything. ​
